Hello fellow, Runner!

Welcome to AUPP Running website

We are the heartbeat of campus, one stride at a time. More than just a running club, we are a community of students, faculty, and staff united by a shared passion for movement, wellness, and connection. Whether you're training for your first 5K or your next marathon, chasing a personal record, or simply seeking a scenic sunset jog with great company, you have a place here. We believe running is a powerful tool for mental clarity, physical resilience, and building lasting friendships. Our club welcomes all paces and backgrounds, fostering an inclusive, supportive environment where every runner is celebrated. Join us on the pavement, the trails, and in camaraderie—let’s run this journey together.
